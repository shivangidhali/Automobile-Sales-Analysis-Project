# Automobile-Sales-Analysis-Project

## Project Overview:
A Power BI dashboard analyzing automobile sales to track revenue, sales trend, and performance metrics. Include insights on geography, product segments, and brand performance. Designed to support data-driven business decisions.

## Objective:
The Goal of this project: Analyze and Understand Brand performance, sales, unit-sold and analyze total costs, total revenue, losses rates for Discount, sales trends, Product quantity, Pricing and Discount Analysis etc.

## Problem statement:
- Analyze Yearly Revenue trend
- Top 6 country by Revenue
- Analyze segment and country by Revenue
- Analyze Revenue by Brand and Model
- Which segment each brand focuses on?
- Analyze average price of USD by Revenue
- Analyze Total Revenue and quantity by Model
- Analyze Brand wise Discount %

## Key Findings and Insights:
- Best performing segment is Truck with $1,377.5 Revenue
- Highest revenue Month is January 2022 at $83.4M
- SUV is underperforming with only $1361.9M revenue
- Maxico is leading with highest performance $377.5M revenue
- India showed highest growth at 1.8%
- Australlia is lowest with $352.4M, Needs attention
- Toyota is leading top performer with $570.2M revenue
- Model D is best selling with $1105.0M revenue
- BMW is underperforming with $535.9M
- Ford has lowest discount at 7.4%
- Tesla is giving highest discount at 7.5%
- After discount there are $400.3M lost revenue

## Recommendation:
- Focus more on Truck segment to maximize revenue
- Improve SUV performance using pricing or feature strategy
- Expand in Mexico and invest more in India (high growth)
- Fix Australia with targeted marketing and pricing
- Support Toyota and improve strategy for BMW
- Promote Model D (best-selling product)
- Optimize discounts to reduce $400M revenue loss

## Conclusion:
Truk segment and Model D are driving the majority of revenue, while SUVs and markets like Australia need improvment. High-growth regions like India and strong markets like Mexico offer expansion opprtunities. Optimizing discount strategies can significantly reduce revenue loss and improve profitability.
